Pug SDK — Privacy Controls

Demonstrates the two integrator-owned PII controls: the data-pug-no-capture attribute and the sanitizeUrl init hook. The SDK runs in dryRun mode here, so nothing is sent.

1. data-pug-no-capture — redact element text

Click the buttons. Captured click text appears below. Buttons inside a marked region report redacted text (the click still counts — only the text is dropped). The unmarked button reports its real text.

2. sanitizeUrl — mask routes & strip PII params

The same maskUrl function passed to init({ sanitizeUrl }), applied to sample URLs. It runs on $url and $referrer on every event, and on a form's action when the form is submitted.
